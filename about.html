About FortInternational Academy

Fort International Academy journey towards education excellence began in 2004 with an ideal mix of academic and corporate professionals Who felt the shared need to provide inclusive education.We Challenge our Students with a unique educational experience which emphasises a seemless blend of academic,sports, performing arts and experimental learning programes . Fort international Academy, is a lush green, pollution free 80-Acr eco-friendly Campus in pahala hill station in kolhapur, Maharashtra ,India is co-educational residential School affiliated to Central Board of Secondary Education, Delhi ( CBSE) and Secondary School Certificates (SSC) for students up to grade 1st to 12th.
