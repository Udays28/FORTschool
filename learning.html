Research based learning

In a research-based learning is a innovative approach , students actively search for and then use multiple resources, materials, and texts in order to explore important, relevant, and interesting questions and challenges. They find, process, organize and evaluate information and ideas as they build reading skills and vocabulary. It integrates learning, teaching, and research, emphasizing active student engagement. Instructors facilitate RBL by presenting topics as open questions, encouraging students to explore and discover knowledge through research.
